TikTok LIVE can be a powerful way to grow your streaming audience, build hype, and direct traffic to your Twitch, Kick, or YouTube.

üßæ TikTok LIVE Eligibility Requirements

To go live on TikTok, your account must meet these criteria:

Requirement Details
Age Must be 18 years or older
Followers Must have at least 1,000 followers
Region LIVE is not available in every country yet
Community Guidelines Your account must be in good standing

‚úÖ Once eligible, a "Go LIVE" button will appear in the mobile app.

üì¶ Two Types of TikTok LIVE Streaming

1. Mobile Streaming (Native Method)

Go live directly from your phone.

Steps:

  • Open the TikTok app
  • Tap the "+" (Create) button
  • Swipe to LIVE
  • Enter a title, choose a cover photo
  • Tap Go LIVE

üéØ Best for IRL content, chatting, mobile games

2. Stream Key Access (RTMP - for OBS/PC setup)

Stream from OBS or Streamlabs to TikTok using a stream key.

Note: This is available via:

  • TikTok LIVE Studio (Beta)
  • The TikTok LIVE Access program (requires approval or agency partnership)

‚úÖ If you have access to stream key:

Steps to Go LIVE via OBS:

  • Get your TikTok Stream Key + RTMP Server URL
  • Open OBS or Streamlabs
  • Go to Settings > Stream > Custom
  • Enter your RTMP server and stream key
  • Add your scenes, webcam, overlays
  • Hit Start Streaming

üí° Use 9:16 vertical canvas (e.g., 1080x1920) for best results

üì¢ Best Practices for TikTok LIVE

  • Add a strong thumbnail and vertical layout
  • Engage in chat frequently ‚Äì reply to comments in real time
  • Promote your stream beforehand using TikTok Stories or posts
  • Add bio links to your Twitch/YouTube for traffic funneling
