Streaming Accounts

Your complete guide to setting up accounts

Choose Your Platform

Selecting the right platform is your first step to streaming success. Each platform has its own unique features and community. Here's what you need to know:

Twitch

Perfect for gaming, creative content, and building a dedicated community

YouTube

Ideal for both live streaming and on-demand video content

Kick

Great for streamers looking for higher revenue share and new opportunities

Facebook Gaming

Excellent for reaching your existing Facebook audience and casual viewers

Account Creation Process

Creating your streaming account is straightforward, but there are some important steps to follow. Here's your step-by-step guide:

Email Setup

Create a professional email address that represents your brand.

Pro Tip

Use a custom domain for your email to look more professional

Username Selection

Craft a standout username that embodies your brand and is available on every platform you plan to use.

Pro Tip

Keep it short, memorable, and easy to type

Password Security

Create strong, unique passwords for each platform.

Security

Combination between letters, numbers, and other special characters

Account Verification

Complete the email and phone verification process to secure your account and enable all platform features.

Important

Keep your phone number updated for security alerts

Profile Setup

Your profile is your streaming identity. Make it count with these essential elements:

Profile Picture

Upload a high-quality, recognizable image that represents your brand. Recommended size: 800x800 pixels.

Channel Banner

Design an eye-catching banner that showcases your content style. Recommended size: 1920x480 pixels.

Channel Bio

Write a compelling description that tells viewers what to expect from your content and when you stream.

Social Links

Add links to your social media platforms and other content channels to help viewers find you everywhere.

Platform-Specific Requirements

Each platform has unique requirements to ensure your account is fully functional. Here's what you need to know:

Twitch

  • Enable two-factor authentication for enhanced security
  • Complete streamer verification process
  • Set up your stream key in broadcasting software

YouTube

  • Verify your account for live streaming access
  • Enable monetization if eligible
  • Set up your channel customization

Kick

  • Set up your stream key
  • Complete identity verification for payouts
  • Configure your stream settings

Facebook Gaming

  • Connect your Facebook gaming account
  • Set up your gaming page
  • Configure your stream settings
