Basic Overlay

Learn how to create and set up professional stream overlays

Stream overlays are visual elements added to your broadcast to provide information, branding, and interactivity. They can include webcam frames, alerts, chat boxes, and more. Setting up overlays can seem daunting at first, but with the right tools and guidance, it's a straightforward process.

üß∞ Essential Components of a Stream Overlay

  • Webcam Frame: Surround your webcam feed with a custom border to give it extra visual impact.
  • Alerts: Notifications that appear on-screen when viewers follow, subscribe, donate, etc.
  • Chat Box: Displays your chat on the stream, allowing viewers to see interactions.
  • Stream Labels: Showcases recent followers, top donors, or other dynamic information.
  • Panels and Banners: Used for information like "Starting Soon," "Be Right Back," or "Stream Ending."

    • Image: For static overlays like webcam frames.

  • Arrange and Resize: Position and resize your sources to fit your desired layout.
  • Save and Test: Once set up, do a test recording or stream to ensure everything appears as intended.
