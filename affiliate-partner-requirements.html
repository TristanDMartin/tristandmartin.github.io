Twitch Affiliate

Learn about the requirements and steps to achieve Twitch Affiliate and Partner status

Twitch Affiliate Requirements

To become a Twitch Affiliate, you need to meet all of the following within a 30-day period:

50 Followers

Gain at least 50 followers on your Twitch channel

Pro Tip

Engage with other streamers and communities to grow your follower count organically.

500 Total Minutes

Stream for at least 500 total minutes (8 hours & 20 minutes)

Pro Tip

Break this into multiple sessions - consistency is more important than marathon streams.

7 Unique Days

Stream on at least 7 different days within the 30-day period

Pro Tip

Aim for 3-4 days a week to easily hit this requirement and build consistency.

3 Average Viewers

Maintain an average of 3 viewers across all your streams

Pro Tip

Engage with your audience constantly, even if it's just one person watching.

Steps to Achieve Affiliate

1

Stream Consistently

Aim for 3-4 days a week to hit the 7-day minimum and build a regular audience.

2

Engage Your Audience

Talk to your viewers constantly, even if it's just one person. Interaction builds loyalty.

3

Promote Your Streams

Share on Discord, Twitter/X, Instagram, TikTok, and join communities to grow your reach.

4

Focus on Quality

Good mic, webcam (optional but helps), and overlays boost retention and professionalism.

5

Use Twitch's Built-In Tools

Set up panels, About Me section, and chat commands to enhance your channel.

Important

Once you meet the requirements, Twitch will email you and you'll see a notification on your Creator Dashboard to accept the Affiliate Agreement and set up your payout method.

Twitch Partner Requirements

To become a Twitch Partner, Twitch looks for more consistent growth and stronger community engagement.

25 Average Viewers

Maintain an average of 25 viewers across all streams, excluding raids

Challenge

This is the most challenging requirement - focus on community building and retention.

12 Unique Days

Stream on at least 12 different days within the 30-day period

Pro Tip

Consistency is key - maintain a regular streaming schedule.

25 Hours Streamed

Stream for at least 25 total hours across those 12+ days

Pro Tip

Quality over quantity - focus on engaging content rather than just hitting time targets.

⚠️ Important Note

Reaching these requirements doesn't guarantee Partner status—it allows you to apply. Twitch manually reviews applications and looks for community engagement, consistency over time, and unique content or brand.

Tips to Reach Partner

Build a Community

Focus on building a community, not just an audience. Discord helps retain and build loyalty among your viewers.

Network with Other Streamers

Collaborations and raids from similar-size streamers can significantly grow your numbers and introduce you to new audiences.

Analyze Metrics Weekly

Use Twitch Analytics to understand what's working and double down on successful content and strategies.

Create Off-Platform Content

TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Twitter/X drive new traffic to your Twitch channel and help build your brand.

Maintain a Schedule

Reliability is key. Viewers come back when they know when you're live and can plan around your streams.

Focus on Quality

Invest in good audio, video quality, and overlays. Professional presentation helps retain viewers.
