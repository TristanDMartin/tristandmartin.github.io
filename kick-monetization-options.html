Kick Monetization Options

Earn More. Keep More. Grow Your Streaming Career.

95% Revenue Split
$50 Min Payout
24/7 Support

Kick is known for its 90/10 revenue split and lower payout thresholds, making it one of the most streamer-friendly platforms when it comes to monetization.

🔓 Who Can Monetize on Kick?

Kick makes it easy — no partnership or affiliate requirement to start earning.

You only need:

  • A verified account (phone + email)
  • A completed payout setup (via Stripe or bank)

Once your account is verified, you can begin accepting Subs, Tips, and more!

🤑 1. Subscriptions (The Main Monetization Method)

Kick allows viewers to subscribe to your channel at $5/month.

  • You receive 95% – Kick takes only 5%!
  • That's $4.75 per sub in your pocket.

Sub Perks You Can Offer:

  • Custom emotes (upload in dashboard)
  • Subscriber badges
  • Ad-free viewing
  • Exclusive Discord roles or content (optional)

✅ Set up via your Kick Creator Dashboard > Channel Settings > Emotes & Badges

💸 2. Tips (Direct Donations)

Kick allows direct tipping via third-party tools:

  • StreamElements (Tip Page)
  • Botrix (Kick Chat Tips)
  • Ko-fi or Buy Me a Coffee

To add a tip panel:

  • Create an external tipping link (e.g., from StreamElements)
  • Paste it in your Kick channel bio using the +Add Panel option

✅ Tips are instant and have no platform fees (only processor like Stripe/PayPal).

🎁 3. Kick Creator Program (Coming Soon / Beta)

Kick has teased a Creator Program for monetizing based on:

  • Hours watched
  • Viewer engagement
  • Stream frequency

This is still in beta, but expect options similar to Twitch's Partner Plus or YouTube's Partner Program in the future.

📈 4. Sponsorships, Affiliates, and Merch

Kick allows total freedom — you're not locked into exclusive contracts.

Ways to monetize outside the platform:

  • Partner with brands (e.g., GFuel, NordVPN, etc.)
  • Promote affiliate links (Amazon, gaming gear, etc.)
  • Sell merch via platforms like Fourthwall, Streamlabs Merch, or Spring

Add banners, panels, or nightbot commands to showcase your offers.

📊 Payout System

Kick uses Stripe for fast and transparent payments.

  • Minimum payout: $50
  • Payout schedule: Daily, weekly, or monthly (customizable)

✅ Go to Dashboard > Settings > Payouts to configure your account.
